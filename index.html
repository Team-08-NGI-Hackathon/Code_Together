Mentorship Program

The mentorship program will pair youth with another teen with experience in technology who can act as a guide, a friend and a coding buddy for students. This allows students who may not have access to these opportunities to receive one-on-one help and get a taste for what technology is all about.

School Program

Code Together also works with schools to widen the scope of opportunity to more youth. Schools can contact us to set up a representative to speak at an assembly igniting the interest of coding in young children. If there is enough student interest, schools can then adopt this program and form a club for students to participate in.

Network with Others!

Through Code Together, youth across Canada are able to connect with mentors who share similar interests and come from similar backgrounds in a safe space. Youth will be able to learn, discover and inquire about opportunities and careers in technology. Mentors will be able to provide valuable information and help in topics ranging from entrepreneurship in tech, and will be able to connect youth to programs and opportunities.
